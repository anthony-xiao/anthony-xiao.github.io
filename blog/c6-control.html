When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

During programming one trigger that can get an emotional reaction from me will be finding myself missing the obvious. If I am stuck and having to find help whether online or from someone else, when I see the answer and it is quite obvious and something I know, it makes me think why did I not think of that. Otherwise in general I am pretty calm and most situations and try to take on challenges even keeled.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

After trying the Siberian North Railroad it felt interesting. I can understand what the exercise is trying to do but I think because of already having done something similar and know what it is trying to achieve made it less effective than it would have. I really do like the exercise and what it tries to do. Maybe also to do with my personality it felt a bit too long. Next time I will try a shorten version see if it will suit me better and get the results. I think this is something very beneficial because I have seen many who have been effected by their emotion and as a result hurt their work and performance.
