Complete the political awareness exercise here in writing.

My Point of View
The situation was regarding at work me having to fire one of my team members. It was a sitaution where the person was not performing up to the standards that were required. There was change that happened recently but everyone else seemed to have adjusted fine and their results were still at a high standard. This person was also being emotional and is running into conflict with a few others. As a result of this it was only right I took this action for the good of the team.

The Other Point of View
The situation was unfair. There had been change and being here for so long it will take time to adjust. During this adjustment period it should be understandable that some ups and downs would result from it. Also having been here for so long there should be some benefit of the doubt in regrads to work quality and trust that things will eventually come back around. With new leadership there should also be an understanding that not everyone will follow and we should be allow to express that, and doing so shouldn't be condemned.

This exercise was quite nice to do in that in normal cases not many are 100% one way or the other. But doing this allows you to see why certain viewpoints come about and why what you personally see can also have a counter argument that is very valid.
